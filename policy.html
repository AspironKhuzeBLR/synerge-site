Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

  • Personal Information:
    • Account Information: When you create an account, we may ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, and profile information (such as profile picture and bio).
    • Login Credentials: We securely store your login information.
  • Usage Data:
    • Activity Information: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as posts, comments, and likes.
    • Log Data: We collect log data that your device sends whenever you use our App. This log data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device type, operating system, app version, the time and date of your use, and other statistics.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies:
    • We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our App and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our App.
